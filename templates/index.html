Daily Tasks

Daily tasks is a log that tracks tasks or events that occur every day. For most people, these include the time they wake up, the time they go sleep, what meals they had, how much water they've consumed, if they've had their daily doses of supplements etc.

These daily activities are highly individual and no two individuals daily events or events they wish to track are the same therefore it is up to each individual to select what events they wish to track. You can do this by clicking here or by clicking Add Daily Tasks from the navigation bar on top of each page.

Activities

Activities are events that do not occur everyday but you need to be mindful of and remember to complete them on the day. These can be things like birthdays, interviews, assignments that all need to be remembered and marked as completed on the day

These activities are also individual and you can see a list of your activities as well as schedule activities by heading over to the Activities tab or clicking here

Missed completing some tasks? Don't Worry!

The Online Diary also allows for you to go back and complete tasks that you have left blank on previous days for whatever reason. Forgot to pop in what time you went to bed? No problem! Just head over to the Todays Diary tab and select the date for which you still want to complete the missed tasks for!

Once you have completed these tasks you can head over to the logs page and they should appear in your logs!

Completed Logs

The completed logs tab keeps track of all the tasks and activities you have completed in the past so you can always go back and review them. If you wish you can also export these logs into .csv format so you can input them into any of your analysis tools to notice any patterns!

Please click here to view your logs (if you have any!) or click here if you need to add some daily tasks to schedule so you can complete them and view your own personal logs!
